Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how WheelWay uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, improve your experience, and track your activity on the site.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the operation of our website and cannot be disabled. They include, for example, cookies that enable you to log into secure areas of our site.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies help us improve our website by collecting information about how visitors use it, such as which pages are most visited.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences and help us personalize your experience, such as remembering your language preference.
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: These cookies track your browsing habits and may be used to deliver personalized ads or content to you based on your interests.

3. How to Control Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. You can choose to accept or block cookies, delete cookies already stored on your device, or set your browser to notify you when a cookie is being set. Please refer to your browser’s help section for more information on how to manage cookies.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party service providers, such as advertising networks or analytics services, to place cookies on our website. These cookies are governed by the third parties’ respective privacy policies.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our use of cookies, please contact us at info@wheelway.com.
